Palawan, Philippines

By Bree Marr

Why the Philippines?

  1. The Philippines is a wonderful place to travel to. This inexpensive tropical
    beach vacation in Southeast Asia, is where visitors get the chance to explore
    one of the most beaufitful places in the world.
  2. The Philippines are home to the towering limestone mountains that plunge into the
    turquoise sea, making a postcard-worthy backdrop you'll never forget.
  3. Palawan is most famous for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, lush tropical landscapes,
    and picturesque white-sand beaches.

What to do in Palawan?

  1. Underground river tour
  2. serene boat rides
  3. Swim at the white-sand beach in the clear and shallow waters
  4. Try the amazing food, only found in the Phillippines
  5. Learn more about the colorful culture and incredible fesivals of the Phillippnies!

Native Animals:

Palawan is known for their exotic and exclusive animals. Here are some of the most notable;

  1. Palawan Peacock-Pheasant
  2. Palawan Bearcat
  3. Palawan Pangolin

Philippines Culture:

The philippines is home to beautiful and rich culture of combined Asian, European, and
American influences. There is a strong sense of community, creativity, resilience, optimism,
and emphasis on humor and entertainment, making the Phillippines an incredible vacation
spot and a MUST VISIT at least once in your life.
